Leonardo da Vinci

"Art is never finished, only abandoned."

Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519) was an Italian polymath of the Renaissance era, widely recognized for his paintings such as the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper. Apart from being a master artist, he was also an inventor, scientist, and architect whose notebooks reveal a mind centuries ahead of his time. Mysterious smile and expression. Uses sfumato technique – soft transitions between colors/tones. Background landscape is dreamlike and enigmatic. One of the most famous and studied artworks in the world.
